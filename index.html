Game Flow

1. Quiz Round

No. of Questions: 12
Total Marks: 50
Time duration: 5:00 PM – 5:30 PM i.e. 30 minutes(you need to strictly follow the given time window)
Played by: All registered teams

  • All questions will be related to IPL in some way or the other.
  • The top 8 teams will qualify for prediction and auction rounds. Disqualified teams will play the IPO round.
  • In case of a draw in the quiz round, the winner will be decided by backward marking. (Use your entire 30 minutes!)

The quiz solutions and the list of players will be available right after the quiz round along with a 30 minutes break. This is the time to strategise for the Prediction round and IPO round.
The final scorecard will be calculated and shared, and IPO and prediction rounds will start simultaneously.

2. Prediction and IPO Rounds

Prediction round (For quiz qualified teams)

Time duration: 6:00 PM – 6:20 PM
Played by: Top 8 qualified teams
Team of 7 to be predicted by each qualified team before the auction. First 10 minutes for predicting and next 10 minutes for analysing predicted players of other teams.

  • If a team manages to buy any of their predicted players, 10% increase in that player score.
  • If a team is unable to buy any of their predicted players, 15% decrease from the overall team score.
  • If a team is able to buy all their predicted players, 15% increase in the overall team score i.e. 15% increase in every player score rather than 10%.

Think, strategise, and predict your 7 wisely!

IPO round (For quiz unqualified teams)

Time duration: 6:00 PM – 6:20 PM
Played by: Disqualified teams
Every team will be given some initial virtual money. And each player will be given a fixed share price of worth 5.3 Lacs.
The motive of this round is to create a priority list of the players. This should depend on your analysis of which players might get sold above the share price to gain profit. The players will be divided into 3 categories and you must choose:

  • 2 players from category 1
  • 3 players from category 2
  • 2 players from category 3

The team with the highest net worth at the end of the auction will be the winner of this round.
Distribution of shares:
Disqualified teams will have to submit 7 players in a priority order (1 being the highest priority and 7 being the lowest). During the Auction Round, the shares will be sold using the following rules:

  • If only a single team is willing to buy an asset, then 100% shares of that player will be sold to them at its share price.
  • If multiple teams are willing to buy an asset, then only the team which has given higher priority to the asset in their priority list will be sold 100% shares of the asset.
  • If multiple teams are willing to buy an asset and have it at the same priority rank, equal shares of the asset will be sold to them at its share price.

(The above rules are similar to that of the IPO round of Stonks, KPL)
Note: A team buying a player in the auction and a team owning its 100% share should not be confused. Only the sale price matters as both rounds are running independently. Analyse the players and prioritise the order wisely to gain maximum profit!

3. Auction Round

Time duration: 6:30 onwards
Played by: Top 8 teams
No. of IPL players: 60 (Including both Indian and Foreign)

Team details:

  • No. of players: Min - 7, Max - 8
  • Team distribution: Min 4 Batsmen, 3 Bowlers and 1 Wicket Keeper
  • Nationality: Min 4 Indian Players and 1 foreign player should be there in a team.
  • Team budget: Rs 40 Lacs.
Manage your wallet well and make full use of the prediction round.
  • Bidding process will be done on the chat box of Google Meet.
  • The minimum raise in the bid will be 0.5 Lacs till 10 Lacs and 1 Lac after that.
  • Keeping the unstable internet connections in mind, a player will only be sold once all other team leaders write ‘out’. So no need to worry about net issues.
  • Only the team leader will bid and rest team members and other people can witness the bidding wars and real time team updates on the website.
  • The normalized scores of every player has been calculated beforehand using stats of previous 3 year performances(2019, 2018, 2017) and some other important factors (whether a player is a captain, their utility, everything has been taken care of). The normalised scores won’t be shared(ofc).
  • In case a team buys 8 players, only the best 7 players will be considered. If a team is unable to buy 7 players, it will automatically lose the auction.

IMPORTANT : The website link will be provided 10 minutes before the quiz round starts. A Google meet link will be shared after the prediction round and the IPO round for the auction round. Keep your secret passcode (which you will share through the Google form) safe and use it for entering the various rounds. Results will be posted ASAP. Hope to see you in large numbers!
